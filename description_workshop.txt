[h1]About [/h1]

This is a mod to help find items that NPCS sell.


Doing /findItem [item] will try to find which NPC sells the item. You use the OpenCommand keybind to automatically OpenChat with "/findItem" in it.

[h2]Examples: [/h2]
[list]
    [*]"/findItem bubble"
    [*]"/findItem Copper Shortsword"
[/list]
